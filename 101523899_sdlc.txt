Learning DevOps is crucial in the software development cycle as it enhances collaboration between development and operations teams, 
leading to faster and more efficient software delivery. It automates processes such as testing, deployment, and monitoring, 
reducing errors and improving reliability. DevOps practices like CI/CD ensure continuous integration and delivery, 
enabling rapid updates and bug fixes. Additionally, it improves scalability, security, and overall software quality, 
making development more agile and responsive to business needs.
