George Brown College, founded in 1967, is a public institution in Toronto, Canada, offering career-focused programs in business, 
IT, health sciences, hospitality, and more. With three campuses—St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront—it provides hands-on learning 
through industry partnerships and co-op opportunities. The college has a diverse international student community and emphasizes 
practical skills for the job market. Its motto, "You can. We can.," reflects its commitment to student success.
